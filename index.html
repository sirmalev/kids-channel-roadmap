🧠 The Psychology

Why kids ages 2-6 watch, learn, and come back

1Character LoveKids need a "friend" on screen — Woola becomes a trusted companion
2RepetitionSame structure every Short = comfort. Kids feel safe knowing what's next
3Color + SoundBright visuals + catchy tunes trigger dopamine. Brains LOVE it
4Direct Address"Can YOU say RED?" turns passive watching into active participation
5Surprise1 "wow moment" per Short — keeps them coming back for more
